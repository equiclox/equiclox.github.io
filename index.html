This is a sun clock.

  • The sun always rises at 00:00 and always sets at 00:00.
  • There are 12 hours in every day, and 12 hours in every night.
  • The speed of time changes throughout the year, as the days get shorter and longer.
  • In the summer, the night time moves swiftly, as the hours till sunrise are short.
  • In the winter, the night time moves slowly, as the hours till sunrise are long.
  • True midnight (sun at its lowest) and true midday (sun at its highest) are always at 6:00.
